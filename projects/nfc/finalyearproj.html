Portable NFC Speaker

I'm creating a prototype intended to replace the current traditional audio guide systems
used in Museums today, using NFC (Near Field Communication) technology.

This project is an experimental technical research development. The projects aim is to produce a product prototype that can be utilized in museums to enhance the user experience, with the goal of making the experience more immersive and informative.

The project will make use of NFC (Near Field Communication) tags, these tags uniquely identify an audio track on the device. They will be placed around the museum on the information plaques.

The prototype is intended to work by hovering the device over one of the information plaques (which contains a concealed NFC tag) to hear the audio track related to that tag and plaque, the audio track will play additional information (and or sounds, in the case of the Booth Museum bird songs and calls) that isn’t already on the plaque.

I'm currently in contact with the Booth Museum of Natural History who is the project's domain expert.
